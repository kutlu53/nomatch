📖How to Play

  1. Place two phones flat on a table, facing each other
  2. Tap the arrow on both phones to start pairing
  3. Wait for the Bluetooth connection (phones will show a success animation)
  4. Each round, you'll see two images - tap your favorite
  5. After 5 rounds, see your compatibility and optionally share contact info

❓Frequently Asked Questions

Why won't the phones connect?

Make sure both phones have Bluetooth enabled and are placed flat on a table, facing each other. The phones need to be within 1-2 meters of each other.

Do I need an internet connection?

No! Nomatch works completely offline. The phones communicate directly via Bluetooth.

Why do the phones need to face each other?

This is part of the game experience! The app uses motion sensors to verify that both players are sitting face-to-face, creating a more personal connection.

Does the app collect my data?

No. Nomatch is privacy-first. We don't collect any personal data, and there are no accounts required. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I play with Android and iPhone?

Yes! Nomatch supports cross-platform play between iOS and Android devices.

How do I change the color theme?

On the pairing screen, tap the palette icon in the bottom corner to choose from different color themes.

🔧Troubleshooting

Pairing Issues

  • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on both devices
  • Place phones flat on a stable surface
  • Keep phones within 1-2 meters
  • Try restarting the app on both devices

Connection Drops

  • Avoid moving the phones during gameplay
  • Keep phones away from other Bluetooth devices
  • Ensure phones are not in low power mode
