Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Overview

Nomatch is a peer-to-peer game designed to be played offline between two people in the same physical location. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what data we collect.

Data We Collect

Data We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Location data (GPS)
  • ❌ Photos or media
  • ❌ Contacts
  • ❌ Usage analytics
  • ❌ Advertising identifiers
  • ❌ Any data sent to external servers

Data Used Locally Only

The following data is used only on your device and is never transmitted to any server:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Bluetooth To discover and connect with nearby players Not stored
Motion Sensors To detect device orientation for pairing validation Not stored
Device Orientation To verify face-to-face positioning Not stored

How We Use Bluetooth

Nomatch uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to:

  • Discover nearby devices running Nomatch
  • Establish a direct peer-to-peer connection
  • Exchange game data between two connected devices
Important: All communication happens directly between two devices. No data passes through our servers or any third-party servers.

How We Use Motion Sensors

Nomatch uses your device's motion sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer) to:

  • Detect if your phone is placed flat on a surface
  • Verify that two phones are facing each other during pairing

This data is processed in real-time and is never stored or transmitted.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with:

  • Third parties
  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics providers
  • Any external services

Data Storage

Nomatch stores minimal data locally on your device:

  • Theme preferences (selected color palette)
  • No personal data is ever stored

Children's Privacy

Nomatch does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Summary

🔒Nomatch is a privacy-first app:

  • No accounts required
  • No personal data collected
  • No internet connection required
  • No tracking or analytics
  • All data stays on your device
